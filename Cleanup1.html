Glenn W. Daniel/King Estate Open Space Park Clean up

November 15, 2014


A hardy crew of volunteers concentrated on the southern section of park lands and collected
huge piles of debris.


A special thanks to all the volunteers; Peet's Coffee; the OKNIA Park Committee;
and Oakland Public Works Environmental Services, for tools and picking up debris.
